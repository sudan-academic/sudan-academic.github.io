I currently hold a position as a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) at the Shanghai Film Academy of Shanghai University, while simultaneously fulfilling the responsibilities of a Post-doc at the FVL Lab of Fudan University, under the guidance of supervisor Prof. Yu-Gang Jiang. I received my Ph.D. degree from East China Normal University under the supervision of Prof. Lizhuang Ma. I also closely collaborate with Prof. Yanwei Fu and Prof. Guangnan Ye. My research interests include computer vision, computer graphics, and human-machine interaction.

I am super enthusiastic about exploring and applying computer technologies in Motion Pictures, Art, and Games! I am looking for self-motivated students. If you are highly creative, have strong coding skills, and are interested in joining my group, please email me your CV!
